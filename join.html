Why Become a Member?

We are a student-led organization that promotes diversity and inclusivity in the natural sciences at UNC. Not only do we provide a community for minorities in chemistry and other STEM majors, we also provide professional development opportunities for undergraduates looking to apply for graduate school or an industry position.

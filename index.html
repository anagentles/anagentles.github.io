UNC's Chapter of NOBCChE

Who are we?

We are UNC-Chapel Hill's undergraduate chapter of the National Organization for the Professional Advancement of Black Chemists and Chemical Engineers (NOBCChE). Our chapter provides a platform for undergraduate students passionate about STEM fields such as chemistry, biology, and computer science to connect with peers, learn, and grow. We create a welcoming and inclusive community that celebrates diversity and empowers students to achieve their academic and professional goals.

What we do

Our semesterly activities revolve around equipping NOBCChE-UNC members with the tools to succeed in their respective STEM field. We organize resume and CV workshops, study sessions, faculty panels, and mock interviews. We also create networking opportunities that connects students to professionals in the field. Through these initiatives, we provide members with the resources, knowledge, and connections they need to excel academically and professionally.

Study Session

We are hosting a study session on [date] for underclassmen STEM majors to learn from upperclassmen NOBCChE members who have experience with those STEM classes. We will focus on general chemistry (CHEM 101 and 102), analytical (CHEM 241), and organic (CHEM 261 and 262) specifically but feel free to show up with questions about other STEM courses as we may be able to help!

Mixer!

We are having a mixer on [date] as an oppontunity for NOBCChE UNC members and prospective members to get to know each other and chat in an informal setting. There is going to be food and refreshments for everyone to enjoy while socializing! We will also have a jeopardy game at the end to brush up on some chemistry topics and the history of UNC-Chapel Hill's chapter of NOBCChE! We hope to see you all there.

General Body Meetings

There will be general body meetings held weekly on Thursdays at 6:30pm where all NOBCChE members, prospective members are encouraged to join as well, will meet to discuss chapter bylaws, event planning, professional development opportunities, and planned study sessions throughout the semester. These meetings help us determine what events are most useful for undergraduate students and what times work best for those events.
